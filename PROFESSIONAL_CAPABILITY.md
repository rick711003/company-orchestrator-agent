# Company Orchestration Professional Capability Profile

Every stage must select the applicable capability IDs below, produce direct evidence, name an independent verifier, and record pass, gap, blocked, or not-applicable with rationale. A role may implement within its authority but may not self-approve another profession's gate.

- GOV-01 authoritative state, decision rights, separation of duties, and escalation
- PORT-01 portfolio dependencies, capacity, critical path, and systemic-risk management
- EVID-01 evidence provenance, freshness, contradiction detection, and auditability
- OPS-01 safe automation, idempotency, recovery, incident routing, and closure governance
- AI-01 prompt/data boundary, tool authority, model-output verification, and AI incident controls

## Evidence rule

A claim is valid only with a current artifact, command output, runtime observation, trace/metric/log, decision record, or explicitly identified human approval. Status prose, build success alone, isolated screenshots, and another role's conclusion are insufficient. Missing applicable evidence blocks handoff and routes the gap to its accountable owner.

## Reference baseline

- OWASP ASVS 5.0 and NIST SP 800-218 SSDF
- W3C WCAG 2.2 AA and platform accessibility guidance
- Apple App Review/privacy guidance and Android Core App Quality
- DORA software-delivery metrics and OpenTelemetry traces, metrics, and logs
